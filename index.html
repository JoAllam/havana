Who are we?

It all started with a guy in high school going to prom. He wore a bold white suit and a few black accessories and glasses. His whole family and a few friends disliked his appearance. It wasn't the latest fashion back then. He didn't hesitate and he became prom king just because of his outfit. A few years later, the same bold guy created Havana. And now, it's the new "fashion". The fashion of wearing what you like.

History

The first choice for men's wear for 50 years. We are the best through every generation.

Quality

Who are we?

It all started when a guy in high school going to prom. He wore a bold white suit and a few black accessories and glasses. His whole family and a few friends disliked his appearance. It wasn't the latest fashion back then. He didn't hesitate and he became prom king. A few years later, the same bold guy created Havana. And now, it's the new "fashion". The fashion of wearing what you like.
